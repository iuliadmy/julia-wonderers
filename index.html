About

Hi, I'm Julia.
We live in the world.

I'm a nomadic mom of two, documenting life on the move — the beautiful, the chaotic, and the deeply real. We've navigated airports at 3am with a toddler, homeschooled in six countries, and learned that the best education doesn't fit inside four walls.

This space is where I share everything I wish someone had told me before our first trip. Honest tips. Free printables. No gatekeeping.
